GET TO KNOW US

Alaz Hub is a South Sudan based information technology company founded in May 2020 by a team of creative and innovative young South Sudanese.Alaz Hub’s foundation is inspired by a need to make information technology and related technologies accessible and available to South Sudanese ventures/companies.

Our mission is to enable innovators, creators and entrepreneurs to strengthen their ventures so as to maximize their returns by offering cost-effective and up to date technology consultancy services.We believe that by empowering these agents, we are able to develop and transform larger communities as they are able to maximumly deliver crucial offerings to, and within our communities

To be the ICT Company of choice in by providing quality service and timely solutions.

Our aims and objectives are to become a power house in terms of converting ICT into an exciting business tool for the community/SME’s/etc in the region. Our objective is to transform your business into a profitable venture for you.
